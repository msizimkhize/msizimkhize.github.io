About myself

Msizi Mkhize is a student of BSc Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of South Africa.

Msizi is also a student of Software Engineering at ALX Africa, where he holds a scholarship, and a Junior Analyst and student of Google Data Analysis, under Umuzi South Africa, where too he holds a scholarship. He was the winner of the Department of Water Affairs and Sanitation merit bursary, through which he studied BEng Mechanical Engineering at the University of Pretoria.
